"Echoes of the Manor"

Hunters

Galatea Claude
( Sculptor)

"Perfection is pain… and I sculpt only the finest forms."

Backstory
Once a prodigy in the world of sculpture, Galatea’s hands breathed life into cold marble, crafting masterpieces that captivated all. But when tragedy struck, her own body was left broken, her dreams shattered alongside it. Desperate to reclaim her lost artistry, she turned to a new medium—one that screamed, bled, and begged for mercy. Now, as a hunter of the Manor, she carves beauty from agony, her statues eternal reminders of suffering.
The Will Brothers
( The Breaking Wheel)

"No escape… only the turning of the wheel."

Backstory
Once enforcers of brutal justice, the triplets who became The Breaking Wheel thrived in the art of punishment. Bound together by fate and cruelty, their executioner's wheel crushed both bones and spirits alike. When their reign of terror ended in betrayal, they were left to rot, forgotten by the world they once ruled. Now, as hunters of the Manor, they continue their twisted duty, ensuring that none escape the relentless cycle of suffering.
Grace
(Naiad)

"The waters whisper… and none can escape their embrace."

Backstory
Once a graceful swimmer, Grace's life was devoted to the sea, her movements fluid as the waves themselves. But when betrayal drowned her in sorrow, the waters answered her grief, pulling her into their cold, eternal depths. Now, as a hunter of the Manor, she glides through the battlefield like a phantom, her aquatic embrace dragging victims into a fate as inescapable as the tides.
Philippe
( Wax Artist)

"Perfection is eternal… let me preserve you forever."

Backstory
Once a renowned sculptor, Philippe turned to wax to capture fleeting beauty, believing only his art could defy time. But when his obsession grew, so did his desire to preserve not just art—but life itself. Now, as a hunter of the Manor, he seals his victims in molten wax, ensuring their beauty lasts for eternity, frozen in a moment of terror.
