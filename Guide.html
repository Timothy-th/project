Identity V Gameplay Guide

Introduction

Identity V is an asymmetrical horror game developed by NetEase.
It features a 1v4 format, where one player takes the role of the Hunter,
while four others play as Survivors.
The goal of the Hunter is to eliminate all Survivors,
while the Survivors must decode ciphers to escape.

Survivor Gameplay

Objective

  • Decode five cipher machines and then open the exit gate to escape.
  • Avoid being captured by the Hunter.

Basic Controls

  • Movement: Use the joystick to move.
  • Interaction: Tap to decode, vault, rescue, or heal.
  • Ping System: Use quick messages to communicate with teammates.
  • Hiding: Hide behind obstacles or in lockers to evade the Hunter.

Decoding Cipher Machines

  • Survivors must decode five cipher machines before escaping.
  • Press the skill check button when prompted to avoid calibration failure.
  • Failed calibrations alert the Hunter to your location.

Kiting the Hunter

  • Kiting means leading the Hunter around to waste their time.
  • Vault windows and pallets to slow the Hunter.
  • Use character abilities (e.g., Forward’s dash, Priestess’s portals).
  • Mind-game the Hunter by faking vaults or juking.

Rescuing Teammates

  • When a Survivor is placed on a rocket chair, you have a limited time to rescue them.
  • Avoid rescuing too early or too late.
  • Some Survivors, like Mercenary and First Officer, are better suited for rescues.

Survivor Roles

  • Decoder: Faster cipher decoding (e.g., Mechanic, Prisoner).
  • Rescue: Strong rescuers with stun abilities (e.g., Forward, Mercenary).
  • Support: Helps teammates with buffs (e.g., Priestess, Entomologist).
  • Kiter: Good at distracting the Hunter (e.g., Seer, Coordinator).

Endgame and Escape

  • Once five ciphers are completed, Survivors must open exit gates.
  • Be cautious, as the Hunter will guard the gates.
  • If three Survivors are eliminated, the dungeon (hatch) opens for a last chance escape.

Hunter Gameplay

Objective

  • Capture and eliminate all four Survivors.
  • Stop them from decoding cipher machines.

Basic Controls

  • Movement: Use the joystick to move.
  • Attack: Tap to hit a Survivor.
  • Abilities: Each Hunter has unique skills to capture Survivors.
  • Chairing: After downing a Survivor, pick them up and place them on a rocket chair.

Chasing Survivors

  • Predict Survivor movements.
  • Use abilities to slow them down.
  • Mind-game Survivors by faking attacks or switching directions.
  • Break pallets and vault windows if necessary.

Camping and Patrolling

  • Camping: Staying near a chaired Survivor to prevent rescues.
  • Patrolling: Moving between cipher machines to stop decoding.
  • Some Hunters excel at camping (e.g., Guard 26), while others are better at map control (e.g., Wu Chang).

Hunter Roles

  • Chaser: Specializes in high-speed chases (e.g., Hell Ember, Geisha).
  • Map Control: Can move quickly across the map (e.g., Wu Chang, Dream Witch).
  • Camper: Strong at guarding chaired Survivors (e.g., Guard 26, Bonbon).

Endgame Strategy

  • Once Survivors finish decoding, block the exit gates.
  • Try to down at least one Survivor before they escape.
  • If three Survivors escape, the last Survivor can find the dungeon to escape.

Game Modes

  • Quick Match: Casual 1v4 matches.
  • Ranked Match: Competitive mode with rank progression.
  • Duo Hunters: 2v8 game mode where two Hunters work together.
  • Custom Mode: Play with friends and adjust game settings.

Tips and Tricks

Survivors

  • Stick to your role (decoding, rescuing, kiting).
  • Use communication pings to alert teammates.
  • Rotate between cipher machines to avoid being found.
  • Always have an escape route when decoding.

Hunters

  • Learn your Hunter’s abilities and playstyle.
  • Predict Survivor movements and don’t always swing immediately.
  • Patrolling cipher machines is key to preventing decoding.
  • Don’t chase a single Survivor too long; pressure other Survivors instead.

Mastering Identity V takes time and practice.Whether you play as a Survivor or a Hunter,
learning game mechanics, character abilities, and strategies
will help you improve. Good luck in the manor!
