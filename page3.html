"Echoes of the Manor"

Survivors

Emily Dyer
( Doctor)

"Don’t be afraid… I’ll do everything I can to save you."

Backstory
Once a skilled physician, Emily Dyer dedicated her life to healing the sick and wounded. With a steady hand and a kind heart, she treated countless patients, earning their trust and admiration. However, whispers of her true identity began to spread—Emily was not a licensed doctor but a self-taught healer with a mysterious past. Fleeing from those who sought to expose her, she found herself at Oletus Manor, where her medical skills became both a blessing and a curse in the endless game of survival.
Freddy Riley
( Lawyer)

"The law is on my side… or at least, it used to be."

Backstory
Once an ambitious lawyer, Freddy Riley built his career on sharp arguments and well-crafted lies. He had a talent for twisting the truth, ensuring his clients walked free—no matter their crimes. But when he defended the wrong person, a powerful enemy turned the tables, leaving him disgraced and hunted. Seeking refuge, he arrived at Oletus Manor, hoping to escape his past. Yet in a place where rules mean nothing, even the best defense may not be enough to save him.
Kreacher Pierson
( Thief )

"Quick hands, quicker feet… that’s how you survive."

Backstory
Once a streetwise pickpocket, Kreacher Pierson made a living slipping through crowds and vanishing without a trace. He never stole for greed—only enough to get by. But one night, his luck ran out. Caught in the act, he was given a choice: face the authorities or take a mysterious invitation to Oletus Manor. Believing he could outsmart whatever lay ahead, he chose the latter. Now, in a place where danger lurks at every turn, his skills may be the only thing keeping him one step ahead of fate.
Emma Woods
( Gardener)

"A flower cannot bloom in chains… but will it wilt searching for the past?"

Backstory
Once a sheltered girl raised under strict discipline, Emma Woods spent her childhood longing for warmth from her father, a gardener who mysteriously vanished one day. Left with only memories and unanswered questions, she set out to find him, following the faintest traces of his past. Her search led her to Oletus Manor, a place where lost souls gather and secrets remain buried. Now, as she tends to the overgrown gardens of the estate, she wonders—will she find the answers she seeks, or has she only dug her own grave?
Lucky Guy
( Deduction Substitute)

"Luck has always been on my side… or maybe I just know how to survive."

Backstory
A man of unknown origins, nobody knows where Lucky Guy came from or how he ended up at Oletus Manor. With no remarkable skills, no grand past, and no connections, he was always overlooked. Yet, against all odds, he manages to escape dire situations unscathed time and time again. Some believe he is simply lucky, while others suspect there is more to his survival than mere chance. Whether fate or fortune, one thing is certain—he refuses to go down without a fight.
Naib Subedar
( Mercenary)

"A debt must always be repaid... one way or another."

Backstory
Once a soldier trained in covert operations, Naib Subedar lived by discipline and duty. However, when a mission went awry, he was abandoned by his superiors, left to fend for himself in enemy territory. Betrayed and disillusioned, he deserted the battlefield, offering his skills to the highest bidder. Though he now fights only for his own survival, the weight of past loyalties still lingers. Drawn to Oletus Manor by a letter promising answers, he soon realizes that some debts can never truly be repaid.
Helena Adams
( The Mind's Eye)

"Even in darkness, I see more than most."

Backstory
Once a blind girl shunned by society, Helena Adams possessed an uncanny ability to perceive the world beyond sight. Raised in an orphanage, she relied on her heightened senses to navigate life, but whispers of her strange abilities spread. When a mysterious letter invited her to Oletus Manor, she accepted, believing it to be a chance to prove herself. Now, in the deadly game of survival, her gift may be the key to escaping—or the reason she never leaves.
Margaretha Zelle
( Female Dancer)

"Watch closely… the final act is always the most dazzling."

Backstory
Once a dazzling star of the circus, Margaretha Zelle captivated audiences with her graceful performances. Behind her charming smile, however, lay ambition and resentment. She despised Joker, the clown who stole the spotlight, and sought to have him removed. In a calculated move, she tampered with his performance, causing a catastrophic accident that left him disfigured and cast out. Though she continued to dance, guilt and fear crept into her perfect routine. When rumors of her betrayal spread, she vanished into the night, eventually arriving at Oletus Manor—where the past is never truly buried, and the dance never ends.
Eli Clark
( Seer)

"The future is never set in stone… but the price of foresight is steep."

Backstory
Once a revered oracle, Eli Clark possessed an uncanny ability to glimpse into the future through his bond with his owl. His visions brought him both admiration and fear—those who sought knowledge came to him, but others believed his foresight was unnatural. When a prediction led to the downfall of a powerful figure, Eli became a target of persecution. Forced to flee, he found refuge in Oletus Manor, where his gift became both a blessing and a burden in the endless struggle for survival.
Norton Campbell
( Prospector)

"The pull of fate is stronger than any magnet."

Backstory
Once a devoted miner, Norton Campbell toiled deep underground, seeking fortune in the heart of the earth. But one fateful day, an explosion rocked the tunnels, leaving his crew buried beneath the rubble—only Norton emerged unscathed. Accusations of sabotage followed him, whispers claiming he had set the blast himself to claim the treasure for his own. Hunted by guilt and suspicion, he wandered until he reached Oletus Manor, where the weight of his past clung to him like iron to a magnet.
Patricia Dorval
( TEnchantress)

"Fate’s design is ever-changing… just like my tricks."

Backstory
Once a traveling magician, Morgana sought to master the art of illusion, captivating audiences with mesmerizing performances. However, her talent for deception extended beyond the stage. Using her charms and sleight of hand, she swindled wealthy patrons out of their fortunes, vanishing before they could uncover the truth. Eventually, her luck ran out, and those she had wronged pursued her relentlessly. Seeking refuge, she arrived at Oletus Manor, where her illusions became more than just tricks—now, they were a means of survival.
Mike Morton
( Acrobat)

"The show must go on… even if it’s my last act."

Backstory
Once a celebrated circus performer, Mike Morton dazzled audiences with his daring stunts and fearless tricks. But his ambition led him to push beyond his limits, attempting a dangerous act that ended in disaster. His closest friend, Joker, was left permanently disfigured, and the blame fell on Mike. Overcome with guilt and shunned by the circus, he vanished into obscurity. Now at Oletus Manor, he leaps and tumbles through danger, still trying to outrun the past that haunts him.
Jose Baden
(First Officer)

"Discipline and duty… even in the face of the unknown."

Backstory
Once a decorated naval officer, José Baden had spent years enforcing order on the seas with unwavering discipline. But when a tragic accident during a voyage led to the loss of his crew, whispers of negligence and betrayal spread. Stripped of his title, he wandered in search of redemption, until fate led him to Oletus Manor. Now, in this twisted game, he clings to his sense of duty, hoping to prove his worth once more.
Luca Balsa
(Prisoner)

"Even in darkness, the spark of hope never fades."

Backstory
Once a brilliant electrician, Luca Balsa’s mind crackled with ingenuity, but his experiments often defied ethical boundaries. His obsession with harnessing electricity led to an "accident" that cost his mentor's life, though whether it was truly accidental remains a mystery. Branded a murderer, he was sentenced to prison, where he continued his research in secret. When the opportunity arose, he escaped—only to find himself at Oletus Manor, where his talents would be tested in a far deadlier game.
Melly Plinius
( Entomologist)

"Even the smallest creatures can change the course of fate."

Backstory
Once a brilliant entomologist, Melly Plinius was admired for her research on insects and their behavior. However, behind her gentle demeanor lay a darker truth. Trapped in a suffocating marriage, she saw no escape—until she devised a chilling solution. One night, her husband was found lifeless, his body covered in venomous insect bites. Though no evidence could directly incriminate her, the whispers never ceased. With her reputation in ruins and suspicion growing, Melly vanished into the shadows. Now at Oletus Manor, she wields her knowledge as both a shield and a silent, crawling terror.
Anne Lester
(Toy Merchant)

"Every toy has a story… and some secrets are best left undiscovered."

Backstory
Once a gifted toymaker, Anne Lester’s creations brought joy to children, but they also carried hidden messages for those in need. Her secret work aiding fugitives drew the attention of dangerous individuals, forcing her to flee after a delivery went wrong. Seeking refuge, she arrived at Oletus Manor, where her toys are no longer just for play—they are tools for survival in a game she never intended to join.
