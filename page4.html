"Echoes of the Manor"

Survivors

Emil
( Patient)

"Pain is fleeting… but the past never fades."

Backstory
Once a troubled soul confined to an asylum, Emil longed for a cure to the torment that plagued his mind. Subjected to countless treatments, he endured pain in the name of progress, yet found solace in the presence of a certain psychologist. When the facility fell into chaos, Emil vanished, his fate unknown. Now, within Oletus Manor, he wanders the halls, torn between trust and fear, searching for a truth that may never be uncovered.
Ada Mesmer
( Psychologist)

"Close your eyes… let your mind drift away."

Backstory
Once a respected psychologist, Ada Mesmer sought to unravel the depths of the human mind, using hypnosis to treat trauma and suppressed memories. But her unorthodox methods pushed the boundaries of ethics, leading to a tragic incident where a patient never woke up. With her reputation in ruins, she vanished from the public eye, eventually arriving at Oletus Manor. Here, she continues her work, walking the fine line between healing and control, as she searches for answers hidden within the subconscious.
Orpheus
( Novelist)

"A story unfinished… a fate unwritten."

Backstory
Once a renowned novelist, Orpheus specialized in psychological mysteries, his stories praised for their chilling realism. However, his fascination with the mind led him to dangerous experiments—hypnosis sessions meant to unlock hidden truths. One such session revealed more than he bargained for, blurring the line between fiction and reality. Haunted by fragmented memories and an inexplicable connection to Oletus Manor, he now searches for the missing pieces of his past, pen in hand, as he writes the final chapter of a story that may be his own.
Little Girl-Alice DeRoss
( Journalist)

"The past whispers its secrets… if only we listen."

Backstory
Once a curious and determined journalist, Alice DeRoss sought to uncover the truth hidden within history’s pages. Her pursuit led her to an ancient, mysterious tome that blurred the lines between reality and memory. As she delved deeper, the book’s power consumed her, leaving her trapped in the form of a child. Now, at Oletus Manor, she wanders through time, searching for the truth that once defined her—before it slips away forever.
Joker
( Weeping Clown)

"A broken smile hides the deepest sorrow."

Backstory
Once a circus performer, Joker lived to bring joy to the audience. But as laughter faded and cruelty took its place, he became the target of mockery and scorn. Cast aside and left to wander, he clung to the remnants of his broken dreams. Now, at Oletus Manor, he plays the fool in a game where no one is truly laughing.
Luchino Diruse
( Professor)

"Knowledge is power… but evolution demands sacrifice."

Backstory
Once a brilliant yet controversial scientist, Luchino Diruse dedicated his life to the study of reptilian genetics, obsessed with unlocking the secrets of evolution. His experiments pushed ethical boundaries, and rumors spread of his willingness to use human subjects in his quest for perfection. When one of his experiments went horribly wrong, Luchino vanished, presumed dead. But at Oletus Manor, he emerged again—no longer fully human, but something far more terrifying.
Qi Shiyi
( Antiquarian)

"Every relic holds a story… and some are better left forgotten."

Backstory
Once a dedicated scholar, Qi Shiyi traveled far and wide to uncover lost artifacts and forgotten histories. Her passion for preservation, however, led her to questionable dealings—smuggling relics, forging documents, and deceiving collectors to keep treasures out of unworthy hands. When one of her transactions went awry, she vanished without a trace, only to resurface at Oletus Manor, where every discovery comes at a price.
Frederick Kreiburg
(Composer)

"Every note has meaning… even the ones drenched in sorrow."

Backstory
Once a celebrated musician, Frederick Kreiburg composed symphonies that enchanted audiences far and wide. But as his fame grew, so did the pressure to create perfection. Desperate to surpass his past works, he turned to unethical means—stealing melodies, silencing rivals, and manipulating those who trusted him. When his secrets unraveled, his reputation crumbled, leaving him with nothing but the echoes of his own deceit. Now, at Oletus Manor, his music lingers, a haunting melody of ambition and regret.
Richard Sterling
( Knight)

"Chivalry is not dead… but neither am I."

Backstory
Once a nobleman bound by duty, Richard Sterling lived by a strict code of honor. However, his unwavering loyalty led him to fight battles that were never his to win. Betrayed by those he trusted most, he vanished from history, his fate unknown. Now, at Oletus Manor, he remains steadfast, a knight without a kingdom, seeking redemption in a game where honor means nothing.
Matthias Czernin
( Puppeteer)

"Watch closely… the strings tell no lies."

Backstory
Once a gifted boy with a talent for crafting puppets, Matthias Czernin’s creations seemed almost alive. The orphanage he lived in cherished his performances—until his dolls started whispering secrets no one should have known. Fear turned to horror when the orphanage burned down, with Matthias the only survivor. Now, at Oletus Manor, he performs for an unseen audience, his puppets dancing to a tune only he can hear.
Andrew Kreiss
( Grave Keeper)

"The dead keep their secrets… and so do I."

Backstory
Andrew Kreiss spent his life tending to the forgotten, offering solace to those who could no longer speak. As a gravedigger, he found comfort in silence, believing that the dead were far kinder than the living. But beneath his quiet demeanor lay a dark past—one buried along with the graves he so carefully tended. Now at Oletus Manor, he continues to dig, unsure if he is preparing resting places for others… or for himself.
Victor Grantz
( Postman)

"No distance is too great… every letter finds its way."

Backstory
Once a devoted mail courier, Victor Grantz found solace in delivering letters, believing that every message carried a piece of someone's heart. However, his deep bond with his only companion, his dog Wick, became his downfall. Accused of prioritizing animals over duty, he was cast out by his superiors. Wandering aimlessly, he arrived at Oletus Manor, where his deliveries now hold a far greater weight—messages of hope, despair, and secrets that should never be read.
