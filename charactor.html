Hunters

Leo Beck
(Hell Ember)

"The flames took everything… now, I take back what’s mine."

Backstory
Once a loving father, Leo Beck let jealousy consume him, setting his factory ablaze in a fit of rage. But the fire took everything—his wife, his daughter, and his humanity. Now, as a hunter of the Manor, he is doomed to burn in his own regret, forever chasing what he lost.
Joker
(Smiley Face)

"Laugh while you can… the fun always ends in screams."

Backstory
Once a beloved circus performer, Joker lived to make the audience laugh. But after a disastrous stunt left him disfigured, the cheers turned to screams, and the circus cast him aside. Now, as a hunter of the Manor, he finds joy in fear, turning every chase into his own twisted performance.

Backstory
Once a gentle caretaker of the woods, Bane Perez devoted his life to protecting its creatures. But when poachers took away the only being he loved, his sorrow turned to fury. Now, as a hunter of the Manor, he roams like a beast, punishing all who dare trespass.
